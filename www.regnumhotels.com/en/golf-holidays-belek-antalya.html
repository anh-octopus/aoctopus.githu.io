Carya Golf Club and National Golf Club

Read More

Enjoy all inclusive golf holidays in Belek

Guests of Regnum Carya Golf & Spa Resort can enjoy memorable golf holidays at two courses: Carya Golf Club, which is Turkey's first and only golf course allowing nighttime golf with 18 floodlit holes, and Belek's first golf club, the 27-hole National Golf Club.  

Both courses have been constructed by top American international architects and complement extensive practice facilities with a fully serviced Clubhouse. This, coupled with an international management team and staff of PGA golf professionals, ensures that Regnum Carya Golf & Spa Resort can rightly take its place as the leader in Turkish golf.

Carya Golf Club

Belek is Turkey’s premier destination for golf holidays and our Carya Golf Club offers an elegant layout in the traditional English architectural style.

The Championship Golf Course at Carya Golf Club was designed by Thomson Perrett & Lobb Golf Course Architects, which was founded by five-time British Open winner Peter Thompson. Named one of the best 100 golf clubs in the world by several reputable golf publications, Carya Golf Club is among only a few golf clubs in Turkey that can host 18-hole international tournaments.

Carya Golf Club has also become the first floodlit golf club in Turkey. From now on, our guests will be able to enjoy a round of golf day or night.

For more information, please visit www.caryagolf.com.

National Golf Club

The National Golf Club 27-hole Championship Course offers something special for all levels of skill. For those just starting out, there is a professional team of highly trained PGA pros who will look after your swing. Following a lesson or a game of golf, you can take advantage of 40 bay Driving Range, 2 chipping and putting greens as well as practice bunkers. 

For more information, please visit www.nationalturkey.com.
