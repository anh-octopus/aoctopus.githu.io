RATES AND DEPOSITS

Rates are subject to change as a result of changes in your arrival and /or departure dates and room types. All rates are quoted in Euro. The maximum number of guests per room is based upon room types. Please check your room type descriptions for details. Children, 12 years and older, stay as adult. 8 percent VAT is included in room rates. Cribs are free depending on the room type capacity.

CREDIT CARD INFORMATION AND PAYMENTS

A valid credit card is required to secure your reservation. Upon reservation, your credit card will be charged for the amount indicated on the booking confirmation page or email. Debit cards are not accepted. Exchange rates are applicable at time of reservation, but are subject to currency fluctuations and billing may be at the prevailing exchange rate upon completion of your stay. Please consider that all the rates provided on the website are in Euro. The name on your credit card must match the name on your reservation. If it does not, please be aware that your reservation will not be guaranteed and potentially be cancelled.

The credit card must be presented upon check-in, along with a valid photo identification of the cardholder and registered hotel guest. On the day of arrival, Regnum Carya will pre-authorize your credit card to ensure sufficient funds are available to cover the charges for your entire stay. Should the credit card decline, be invalid or no credit card, another form of payment will be required or the reservation may be subject to cancellation without notice.

CHECK-IN AND CHECK-OUT

Check-in time is earliest 2 PM and Check-out time is latest 12 PM. You must be at least 18 years old or stay with your parents to check-in and register for a room. A valid government issued identification or passport must be presented upon check-in.

Should you arrive at the Hotel prior to the normal check-in time, you are advised to approach the Hotel's Reception for assistance in storing your luggage until check-in is available. An early check-in fee, which is 50 percent of your daily rate, will apply for early check-ins. Early check-in is only available on request subject to room availability and occupancy levels. Please contact the Hotel before your arrival for early check-in requests.

For late check-out requests, please check with the Hotel's Front Desk - Reception at least 24 hours prior to your departure regarding availability of late check-out. A half-day room charge may be incurred for late check-out between 12 PM to 6 PM and a full day room charge may be incurred for late check-outs after 6 PM.

SMOKING POLICY

All of the rooms are no-smoking in Regnum Carya. Smoking in indoor spaces is prohibited by local law. Smoking is allowed at designated areas outdoor.

FURTHER INFORMATION

In proceeding with the reservation, you further agree to allow Regnum Carya or any of its affiliated companies to send you information on upcoming events and promotions, and or related information via email address provided. You may unsubscribe to any such email at any time upon receipt of the said email or you may contact us via direct@regnumhotels.com

GENERAL PROVISIONS

By proceeding with the reservation, you further agree and acknowledges that if the reservation is accepted by Regnum Carya, your stay subsequently shall be subject to the Hotel’s standard terms and conditions of stay. Regnum Carya shall be entitled to vary, amend and/or otherwise change these terms and conditions at any time without prior notice. The reservation and the terms and conditions set out hereto shall be governed in accordance with the law of the Turkish Republic.
