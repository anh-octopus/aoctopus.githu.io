Adventure, Pool and much more in our Beach Hotel 

Situated on a private, white sand beach overlooking the Mediterranean Sea in Belek-Antalya, Regnum Carya Golf & Spa Resort offers guests an exhilarating variety of activities. With day and nightlife pursuits for guests of all ages, we ensure a diverse and unique experience to match the best all-inclusive beach hotels in the world. Our heated swimming pools and private beach provide abundant opportunities for water sports or soaking up the dazzling Mediterranean sun. Guests seeking to hone their game or learn a new talent are welcome to select lessons in tennis or golf. If moving to music is more your style, Latin or belly dancing classes are also available. Those with a competitive streak will find sports ranging from table tennis and darts to basketball, shooting, boccia, billiards and more. Keep your Belek-Antalya holiday from turning into a guilt trip by visiting the fitness centre for a personal training session or by taking an Aerobics, Zumba or Pilates course. 

Experience the full range of activities, events and adventure pursuits at our 5-star all-inclusive resort and many other things to do in Belek, Turkey as well.

Shows & Parties
Aquaworld

Offering summer exhilaration for visitors of all ages, Aquaworld is an unforgettable water playground featuring a 2,500-square-metre wave pool, swimming pools, a water games park, a family pool, a speed slide, a tube slide, a crazy river ride, a twisting body slide and a baby pool.

Hours:

Aquaworld is open between May 1 and October 15.

Pools and Beach

Featuring pure white sand imported from Egypt, the 200-metre private beach at Regnum Carya Golf & Spa Resort offers beach chairs, parasols and towels, a pier, pier bar and pier pavilion. 

Guests may also choose from several pools located throughout the resort: 

  • Family pool
  • Adult pool
  • Heated outdoor pool ( 12 months open)
  • Golf residence pools
  • Wave pool
  • Indoor pool
  • Heated pool in SPA center with a heated outdoor area


Hours:

Outdoor pools and the piers are open between May 1 and October 15.

Heated outdoor pool is open whole year (according the weather conditions)

Adventure Park

Excitement awaits at Regnum Adventure Park! Located in our pine forest and designed for people who would like to experience "Adventure in the Trees" on their Belek-Antalya holiday, the park offers climbing walls, freefall, zip line rides and an adventure track. For detailed information, please contact info@regnumhotels.com.
