Welcome to Regnum Carya

Enjoy our all-inclusive luxury Resort in Belek Antalya 

Nestled on an immaculate white sand beach overlooking the Mediterranean Sea, Regnum Carya Golf & Spa Resort is an unparalleled private resort paradise in Belek-Antalya, Turkey. Just 25 minutes from Antalya International Airport and boasting an extensive range of family friendly amenities, this all-inclusive 5-star luxury resort features an exquisite choice of guest accommodation. 

From spacious standard rooms starting at measurements of 62 square metres to four exclusive 250-square-metre villas and a lavishly designed and decorated three-storey 3,500-square-metre Crown Villa, you’ll always delight in the highest level of comfort while making wonderful memories during your stay. Regnum Carya guests  will have complimentary entrance to the biggest theme park of Antalya; The Land of Legends. Guest will enjoy the  All Inclusive concept of Regnum Carya Golf & SPA Resort and will join the cheerful world of the biggest theme park of Antalya throughout their holiday.

Promising an experience on par with the best 5-star resorts in Turkey, Regnum Carya Golf & Spa offers guests the opportunity to swim outdoors throughout the winter and summer months, have a holiday full of sports, enjoy an amazing view of the Mediterranean and pristine pine forests and relish the finest flavours of international cuisine with six à la carte restaurants and a main restaurant. While providing peace and quiet to guests who wish to sunbathe on a private beach featuring white sands, Regnum Carya also provides excitement to thrill-seekers with its watersports pier and Adventure Park, including parcours set in the forest. The 4,800-square-metre Greendoor Spa can give you the ultimate soothing of the soul with organic products, massages and skin-revitalising treatments. Also, the resort features a comprehensive range of family holiday pleasures, featuring an aqua park, a family pool, a wave pool and a relaxation pool.

For golf enthusiasts, guests are invited to play rounds at two different courses. Feel free to try Turkey's first and only golf course allowing nighttime golf with 18 floodlit holes and Belek's first golf club, the 27-hole National Golf Club. With three football fields designed to FIFA standards, Regnum Carya Golf & Spa Resort offers facilities for the training camps of professional football teams as well.

As host of the G-20 World Summit in November 2015, this luxury resort has a world-class 7,500-square-metre convention centre with a private entrance, a 2,100-square-metre pillar-less ballroom and 13 meeting halls teeming with inspiring natural light. 

Regnum Carya Golf & Spa Resort is managed by Carya Turizm Yatırımları A.Ş., an associate of the Öztürk Group of Companies and is Europe's first golf hotel to be awarded LEED Gold Certification.
