Regnum Carya Hotel Convention Centre

Read More

Host your meeting or event in Belek Antalya with us! 

As host of the G-20 World Summit in November 2015, Regnum Carya Golf & Spa Resort is one of the premier conference hotels in Belek-Antalya, Turkey. Our world-class 7,500-square-metre convention centre features a private entrance, a 2,100-square-metre pillar-less ballroom and 13 meeting halls teeming with inspiring natural light. Our setting overlooking a lush pine forest offers an inspiring venue for your hotel seminar, training conference, sales meeting or incentive trip. 

Guests hosting a corporate congress at our 5-star all-inclusive resort in Antalya may choose from an extensive array of conference facilities, encompassing a range of layout configurations. All rooms offer a flexible layout and functional designs equipped with state-of-the-art video, audio and light systems.

For enquiries regarding your upcoming conference, seminar or special event, please contact the Regnum Carya Golf & Spa Sales Department at mice@regnumhotels.com.
